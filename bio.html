About Me

My name is Matt Browne and this is a test website created as part of the Software Engineering pre-work course at Flatiron School. I have previously completed a doctoral degree in music composition at the University of Michigan and work as a freelance composer. I am transitioning to a new career in software engineering as a supplement to my music composition work. I have has music performed by numerous classical ensembles and orchestras.
